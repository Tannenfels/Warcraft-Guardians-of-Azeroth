Features
* Military Overhaul:
   * Six new base unit types: Infantry / Ranged Infantry / Casters / Cavalry / Aerial Infantry / Large Infantry.
   * Ten new special unit types: Elite Infantry / Large Cavalry / Archmage / Undead / Swarm / Thralls / Animals / Dragons / War Machines / Siege Machines.
   * New Warcraft Retinues: 8 generic, 5 tribal, 5 nomadic, 66 cultural, 18 religious.
   * Cultural Buildings: 39 building chains.
   * Terrain static modifiers for all units have been changed to better suit their roles.
   * Units are sorted into three different categories: Shock (Cavalry, Aerial Units) / Melee (Infantry, Large Infantry) / Support (Casters, Archers).
   * Vanilla tactics have been modified to accommodate new units.
   * Vanilla leadership traits have been modified to accommodate new units.
   * Vanilla military technologies have been modified to give bonuses to new unit types. Bonuses have also been re-arranged and technologies have been given more fitting localisations.
* Added new trade routes:
   * Major routes:
      * Lordaeron Trade Route
      * Great Sea Trade Route
      * Gold Road Trade Route
   * Medium routes:
      * Azerothian Trade Route
   * Minor routes:
      * Eastern Sea Trade Route
      * Kaldorei Sea Trade Route
* Added chance of spawning cannibal characters for some cultures like Ogre, Centaur, Magnataur, Troll (not Zandalari), Qiraji and many others. Character with such "cannibal" cultures are more likely to get "Cannibal" trait via "Carousing" focus and Seclusion events.
* Added decision to form the Kor'kron.
* Kaldorei can Order to Take Vows to send character either to the Sisterhood of Elune or the Cenarius Circle and remove from line of inheritance.
* Average Lifespan added in effects of race traits.
* Added Tel'Abim Bananas building chain.
* Generated Families rule is back. Added new default option: Infertile (only Infertile rulers generate families). Female rulers generate families as well as males now.
Interface
* Several new title flags have been added into the game.
* New icons for Fel-tainted (religion of fel orcs), Odyn (religion of vrykul), Ursine (religion of furbolgs), Agamaggan (religion of quilboars), Theradras (religion of centaurs) and Kaldorei (religion of night elves).
* Swapped current and changed Fel and Void corruption in event tooltips.
* Changed color of Kingdom of Booty Bay to a more visible color on the map.
* Remade portraits for Genn, Medivh, and Nathanos.
* Remade portraits for Cenarius, Malfurion, Tyrande, Maiev, and Illidan.
* Added Jaina portraits.
* Replaced vanilla gold sound with WoW sound.
* Added LvL-up sound effect. Ding!
* Added new holding icons for night elves, dryads, frost nymphs and ancients.

Tweaks
* Gave claims on Gnomeregan to Mechagon king.
* Now you can become a Vampire or Death Knight by using Ask for Dark Gift, an ability of the Cult of the Damned.
* Tribal Monarchy barons now become Monarchy when their liege is reformed.
* Purged Entrance to Azjol-Nerub province modifiers.
* Vassals of the Horde and the Alliance can now create new kingdoms via the Found a New Kingdom decision.
* Aliden Perenolde now holds ownership of Strahnbrad and Headlands when he becomes the Syndicate leader.
* Kaldorei females can no longer be druids.
* Kaldorei priests, druids, and Ancients of Lore can now be Court Chaplains of a Kaldorei liege.
* Kaldorei characters can now ask Kaldorei priests to be their mentors.
* New dynasties no longer take the name of baronies they own. This should prevent situations where gnolls take dynastic names like Hogger of Lordaeron.
* Tuskarr and gnolls use dynastic names for primary titles.
* Patriarchs of the Holy Light religion are now called Bishops.
* Evil Governments can no longer can create Merchant Republics by using non-evil characters as their vassals and giving them duchies.
* Death God, Legion, and Shath'yar rulers can now only build trade posts in provinces with the same religion. Other rulers can now only build trade posts in provinces without Death God, Legion and Shath'yar religions.
* Genocide decisions require the control of all baronies of a province.
* Dark (Fel, Necromancy, Void religion groups) and Secular (Arcane, Draconic, Elementalist, Nerubian, Secular religion groups and Voidfarer religion) religions no longer send missionaries via RIP.4100 and ZE.5000 events.
* AI can now pick Heritage and Faith focuses now, but rulers that prefer multiculturalism or multi-religiosity (like the Horde or the Alliance) are not going to pick it.
* You can summon sayaadi via the Summon Demon decision only if your public and true religion is Fel.
* Demons, spawned in the Voice in the Head event chain, no longer stay at your court if your public and true religion isn't Fel.
* C'thraxxi, spawned in the Ancient Creature event chain, no longer stay at your court if your true and public religion isn't Shath'yar.
* Rejuvenated Cho'gall, Gul'dan, and Drek'thar.
* Only rulers with Alliance-related religion and culture can be invited to sail to Kalimdor with the leader of Theramore.

Balance
* Rebalanced adventures:
   * Balanced chance of raiding adventure spawn on vanilla (about 6 adventures in 10 years during first 50 ingame years, about 29 in 50-150 ingame years).
   * Decreased chance of worgen pack spawn (about 2 packs in 10 years).
   * Decreased chance of sea ravager spawn (about 10 ravagers in 10 years).
   * Among all the other sea ravagers, the naga are more likely to spawn after their resurfacing.
* Slowed down the army movement speed to the vanilla value.
* Slowed down the navy movement speed by 33%.
* More factors matter when it comes to the class random level up. Ambitious trait increases the chance. Diseases, Fat, Content, and Malnourished traits decreases it.
* Nerfed Tribal Monarchy modifiers a bit to make it more like an intermediate state between Monarchy and Tribe.
* Stormwind can no longer create the Alliance at the start of the first bookmark.
* Gave titular duchy to Medivh to make him less likely to be vassalized by kings.
* Immortal characters are less likely to get Rabies.
* Made technologies uneven.
* Now you can adjust the size of the invaders in the Game Rules separately.
* The Cenarion Circle and the Sisterhood of Elune no longer require to be druid/priest to join (not rank up), but require 10 Learning or Learning education.
* Members of the Cenarion Circle and the Sisterhood of Elune don't need Learning to become druids/priests.
* Enter the Emerald Dream and Build a Hospital quests give more society currency.
* Lowered opinion of other members in the Cenarion Circle and the Sisterhood of Elune.
* AI members of the Cenarion Circle and the Sisterhood of Elune won't do hostile actions towards other members.
* AI immortal characters are less likely to get Depressed (via 6050 event), Stressed (via 6040 event) traits and PTSD events. Both player and AI immortals are less likely to get Lunatic trait (via 6020 event). Evil characters (characters with Death God, Legion, Shath'yar religions) are likely to get rid of Depressed (via 6051 event) and Stressed (via 6041 event) traits.
* Nerfed Mark of Elune, buffed Mark of Light.
* Nerfed Druid Forms.
* Nerfed prestige and piety of Blackhand because he could invite other clans from the start. Gave more prestige and piety to our beloved Orgrim.
* Purged night elven orders of Cenarion Hold and Silverwing Sentinels.
* Great War, Scourge Subjugation CBs give more score for battles and holding positions.
* The Burning Legion are limited to 3 attempts now.

Features
* Map сhanges:
   * Added Mechagon, the Seething Shore and Wintergrasp.
   * Added new provinces in Dun Morogh, Stormwind and the Redridge Mountains.
   * Reworked the South Kalimdor and many regions in the Eastern Kingdoms: Lordaeron, Khaz Modan, Azeroth and the Baradin Bay.
* Merchant republics:
   * Merchant republics are back.
   * The Trade Coalition and Stratholme start as merchant republics.
   * Now merchant republics hold elections every 12 years.
* Legion tweaks:
   * Small demonic invasions happen more often.
   * The Legion’s armies become stronger with each invasion.
   * Now player can see which provinces can be used to summon the Legion. Plus, these provinces are marked by a "Ley Line" modifier.
   * Artifacts that can be used to summon the Legion are marked as "Powerful Magical Artifact".
   * Legion Invasions should now be more common.
* Technocracy and magocracy:
   * Temple holders of the "Secular" religion group or "Gnomish" culture group have Technocracy as their government.
   * Temple holders of "Arcane" religion group or "Dalaranian" culture have Magocracy as their government.
* Election rework:
   * No more "culturism and faithism" in the Horde and the Alliance. The Horde and Alliance's electors don't care about the religion and culture of their candidates.
   * Reworked the AI’s voting logic. For example, Light followers now prefer to vote for humble, chaste, patient characters while Loa worshipers vote for proud, ambitious and brave ones.
   * Culture and religion now have a greater influence on spawning of characters with certain traits like humble, chaste and patient if it's Light follower or honest and brave if it's orc.
   * Reworked the elections for"Undead Horde", "Demonic Legion" and "Black Pact" governments. Now the electors’ votes aren't equal and depend on martial, intrigue, combat skills, and realm size. The votes of the current ruler and his/her powerful vassals are worth more.
   * Death God worshipers prefer to elect the one who holds Frostmourne.
* Followers of the Primitive religion group can now use the decision, "Perform Sky Burial".
* Man'ari is now the demon form of draenei. Draenei and man'ari can marry.
* The founder of Theramore can now invite one non-independent ruler to sail to Kalimdor.
* Added the "Political Faction" government. This type of government severely limits the top tier liege, thus preserving multiculturalism within the faction.
* Gave small bonuses/maluses to all religions.
* Interracial marriages between orcs, humans, elves and draenei are available. Plus, ogres and orcs are allowed to marry.
* Added Rexxar and Mok'nathar culture.
* Added other historical halfbreeds.
* Many class conditions are visible when you ask character to be your mentor and in your Intrigue Tab. Added decision to hide these decisions.
* Added more recommended characters to the bookmarks.
* "Court Dwarf" title is back. Small humanoids and dwarves (congenital trait) can be appointed.
* Small humanoids and other races can be appointed as "Guardian of the Deep" if you have "Underground City" wonder with the appropriate "Guardian of the Deep" upgrade.
* Added “Building Hospital” and “Barrow Den” quests for the Cenarion Circle.
* Added “Study at the Temple” ability for the Sisterhood of Elune.
* Added game rules to disable spawning of the Scourge, Legion and the Black Empire. If these rules are active, "Exodus of the Horde" and "Founding of Theramore" event chains happen after the year 605. If the Legion is disabled, Ner'zhul starts without being under the Legion’s control.
* The “Stop Invader” CB can be used against evil empires or big evil realms like qiraji.
* Negative fertility of some race traits is replaced with the "Infertile" trait. Long-lived races and immortal characters get it when they have too many children. Non-long-lived races get it when they reach andropause/menopause age.
* After "Exodus of the Horde" or "Founding of Theramore" event chains, a landless non-heir character from your dynasty gets your abandoned titles if you have one to give up. Otherwise, your liege gets these titles.
* Adventurers that settle now become independent instead of becoming your vassals.
* New rulers of Gnomeregan and Ironforge can now instantly propose an alliance to each other via event chain.
* "Burn Evil Being" and "Burn Evil Beings" decisions can be now used to get an excuse to execute your landed vassals. AI just takes their lands via these decisions.
